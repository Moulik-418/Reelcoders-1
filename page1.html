What is an educational reel?
An educational reel is a short video that provides informative and educational content on a specific topic. It is designed to engage and educate viewers in an entertaining way.
How can I upload a reel?
To upload a reel, you need to create an account and log in. Once logged in, you can navigate to the Upload section and follow the instructions to upload your educational reel.
How can I participate in contests?
To participate in contests, you need to create an account and log in. Once logged in, you can browse the available coding contests and click on the Participate button to join.
How are winners selected?
Winners of coding contests are selected based on various criteria, including the quality of their code, adherence to contest rules, and overall performance. The judging process is fair and impartial.
How can I contact support?
If you have any questions or need assistance, you can contact our support team by clicking on the Contact button and filling out the form. We will get back to you as soon as possible.
